BEST CHRISTMAS MOVIES TO WATCH WITH YOUR FAMILY

There is something about watching the movie for the occasion. It gives you a warm relaxed feeling that what is occuring in the movie is occuring presently. You feel the spirit and joy of the season. This kind of scenarios occur in cases like watching Valentine movies on Valentines day, Halloween movies on Haloween, Christmas movies during Christmas season and so on. That is why we put this as part of our inspirations because we want you to feel that warm relaxed feeling. We know all you've gone through, We know that the year has being tough but simply because you are alive today means that you have overcome through the help of Christ which we are celebrating presently (Christmas).
If you are confused on which movie to watch or where to start, We have put down some list of Christmas movies which would be a great start for you, So, pick up the remote and sit at the front seat before the TV and get watching.

1. Jingle All the Way

There's nothing Arnold Schwarzenegger won't do to make sure his son gets the toy of his dreams for Christmas.


2. A Christmas Wish

Single-mom Martha Evans (Kristy Swanson) is praying for a miracle to help family this Christmas after being abandoned by her husband, who took all their cash and left her homeless with the children.


3. Its a Wonderful Life

This 1946 Frank Capra film isn't just one of the best Christmas movies of all time — it's one of the best movies in general. It's a Wonderful Life was nominated for six Academy Awards and ranks #11 on the American Film Institute's original list of the 100 Greatest American Films of All Time.


4. The Santa Clause

When a divorced businessman (Tim Allen) accidentally kills Santa, he must take over the role in this 1994 movie. What happens when you accidentally become the man in the red suit? Tim Allen will keep the whole family in stitches as he attempts to avoid his fate as Santa. And in the sequel, he returns in search of his Mrs. Claus.


5.The Santa Clause 2

If you loved the first Santa Clause movie, be sure to watch the heartwarming sequel which this time has Santa looking for a Mrs. Claus.
Let's face it, sequels are typically not as good as the originals, but the Santa Clause 2 is definitely an exception. If you loved the original Santa Clause, this follow-up about Santa's quest for a wife will have you laughing all the way.


6. The Dog who saved Christmas

This movie is similar to Home Alone, but instead of a clever kid, a dog saves the day.


7. Elf

Even though this movie only came out in 2003, its tale of a human man raised as an elf making his way in New York City has quickly become a holiday classic.


8. Last Christmas

In this 2019 holiday rom-com, Game of Thrones' Emilia Clarke plays a Christmas store shop girl who meets charming Tom, played by Henry Golding. Cue the romance — and a major plot twist at the end!


9. Christmas with the Kranks

In the mood for some light-hearted holiday comedy? This holiday flick follows a couple, played by Tim Allen and Jamie Lee Curtis, who anger their neighbors by planning to skip Christmas in favor of a Caribbean cruise trip.See what happens when Luther (Tim Allen) and Nora Krank (Jamie Lee Curtis) decide to toss Christmas traditions aside and take a cruise in the Caribbean instead.


10. Dr. Seuss' The Grinch

No list of Christmas movies is truly complete without this latest animated version of one of our favorite holiday stories — which has Benedict Cumberbatch taking on the voice of our favorite holiday grump.
With several adaptations over the years, The Grinch is arguably one of the best Christmas movies of all time. Plotting to ruin the festivities for the villagers of Whoville, grumpy Grinch has a change of heart thanks to sweet little Cindy Lou.


There are so many of them but we have chosen a few knowing that you would be satisfied watching them throughout the Christmas Season. Have a Merry Christmas and a Happy New Year!
